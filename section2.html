Section Two

Trust, Truth, and Coping

Broken trust and distorted truths intensify the pain caused by gossip and betrayal. When trust is fractured, the soul struggles to find footing, and coping mechanisms emerge as shields against further injury. This section examines the delicate process of discerning survival from corruption, reclaiming truth from lies, and understanding that coping is not failure. Here, you will learn how to navigate a world that misunderstands your pain, protect your heart, and recognize the spiritual principles that allow restoration to begin.
